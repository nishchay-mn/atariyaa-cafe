About Us

Not just a cafe.
It's a mood.

Nestled in the heart of Malviya Nagar, Atariyaa is where Jaipur comes to breathe. With an open-air setup, eclectic decor, and a menu that blends global flavors with desi soul — every visit feels like a little escape.

Our baristas craft each cup with love, our chefs put heart into every plate, and the vibe? It takes care of itself. Come for the coffee, stay for the atmosphere.
